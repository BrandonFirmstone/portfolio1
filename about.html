About Siana

All about Siana and her art

Get to know me

Hi! As you've probably guessed by the name of the page, my name's Siana. I'm seriously passionate about art - primarily photography and character design. I developed from doodles to bigger and better art. I studied Level 2 Art and Design at Coleg Cambria in North Wales. I love video games, particularly Vermintide, Minecraft and Paladins.


About the Website

This website is for sharing what I've made to anyone that's interested. I'm actively looking for work, whether you're a company needing a character/graphic designer, or an individual wanting to make a commision! Feel free to contact me using the form HERE!



Contact Me




This privacy policy is to confirm with the user of this website, 'Siana's Gallery', the use of the data collected from this privacy policy.

When filling out the contact form on this page, you are supplying your full name, e-mail address, your enquiry and whether or not you consent to this privacy policy. The individual's full name is used in order for the manager of this website to contact the individual directly.

The e-mail address of the individual is collected in order to respond to the enquiry provided later on by the individual filling the form.

Any text and information inputted into the 'Enquiry' box will be seen by the website management. Any of the information in the enquiry box will be used by Siana to respond and will not be shared with anyone outside of Siana's Gallery.

None of the information that is submitted through this form will be shared with any third party. The content of the form is kept secure according to GDPR laws.

Once your enquiry has been answered to the individual's satisfaction the data will be deleted within 14 days unless otherwise stated by staff at Siana's Gallery.

Reasons for keeping your information longer can include due to the collaborative nature of the enquiry or for individuals commisioning art from Siana the e-mail may be required to update the individual on progress.
